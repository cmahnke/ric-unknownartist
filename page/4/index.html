Das Haus der Frau

One of the experts helping me out from time to time is Gerbrand Caspers, also known as The Linosaurus , has started a new project. I’ve been following his work for about a decade and his colloction of woodcut prints is quite impressive. Please have a look at his new project website Das Haus der Frau , he also offeres a printed lexicon containing the results of his research.
