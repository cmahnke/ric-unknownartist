A pseudonym of Hede Klinge-Neumann in the "Catalog of Copyright Entries"

As Mark Strong of Meibohm Fine Arts pointed out, there is also a Pseudonym of Hede Klinge-Neumann in the “Catalog of Copyright Entries” 1939, Vol 34 No 1-4. A the name “Dienhoff” is noted to be a pseudonym of H. Neumann-Klinge.

More about this catalogue can be found in the corresponding article on Fritz Neumann . Other entries mentioning here are in a seperate post .
